Pasta Cosi is an Italian restaurant in Tunis, les Berges du Lac I. Embark on a
gastronomic journey around the flavors of overseas. Our chef offers a variety of pastas,
risottos but also raclette.

WHAT you will taste at our place :


  • Pasta with Parmigiano (White Sauce & Red Sauce).

  • Risottos.

  • Salads.

  • Salads.

Choose your pasta (Spaghetti, Penne, Farfalle, Tagliatelle) and it is served after dipping
in a wheel of parmigiano.
